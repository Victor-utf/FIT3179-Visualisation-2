Companies in the Fortune 500

Although China and the US have the same number of companies in the Fortune 500, the US employs a significantly fewer proportion of its population in these companies.

Most continents companies are dominated by those of a single country, such as the US in North America and China in Asia.

The revenue of the Fortune 500 companies follows a 'power law', with high-ranking companies' revenue far exceeding the low-ranking ones. However, profits do not behave in this manner and are far smaller and more stable
