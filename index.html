Companies in the Fortune 500

Countries in yellow are generally those with no companies in the Fortune 500. It canbe seen that China and central Europe have particularly large Fortune 500 companies' revenue relative to their GDP.

Most continents' companies in the Fortune 500 are from a single country, such as the US in North America and China in Asia.

The revenue of the Fortune 500 companies follows a 'power law', with high-ranking companies' revenue far exceeding the low-ranking ones. However, profits do not behave in this manner and are far smaller and more stable
